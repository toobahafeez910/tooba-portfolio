About Me

Bridging Chemistry & Healthcare

I am a Chemistry graduate and an M.Phil. Biochemistry candidate with a strong passion for pharmaceutical sciences, nanomedicine, and advanced drug delivery systems.

My research focuses on developing liposomal formulations using naturally derived phospholipids, particularly egg yolk lecithin, to enhance the stability, bioavailability, and therapeutic performance of anticancer compounds.

My work includes natural phospholipid extraction, liposome formulation, characterization, scientific literature review, and pharmaceutical formulation research. I am committed to contributing to innovative biomedical research that bridges chemistry and healthcare through advanced drug delivery technologies.
